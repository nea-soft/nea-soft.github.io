About Us

Your travel experience to jodhpur can be enhanced with the service of professional tour guides, who are friends in distance. Jodhpur Guide Association would like to offer it's services to make your trip a memorable one. Our members are trained and understandable.

Jodhpur which lies on the edge of the desert. The entrance to the great Indian thar desert. The former captail of erstwhile Marwar state. Popularly known as Blue city because of its blue painted houses. but for localitie it's "Suncity" which gets the maximum amount of sunlight during summer. The town which is cultural capital of Rajasthan where guest is considered as god. "Padaro Sa" is traditional welcoming word. Marwari language is so sweet that it's considered as soft spoken language like french. known for it unique festival called dhinga Gevar. Known for its innovative delicious foods, popular snacks and mouth watering desert.

Our member have a vast experience of guiding, some of our members are working as a guide since 40 years. Our members are license by the ministry of Tourism Government of Rajasthan and Ministry of Tourism and Culture Government of India. They have attained various refreshers courses organised by the competent authority. Our members have a vast knowledge of history, culture, art and architecture.
